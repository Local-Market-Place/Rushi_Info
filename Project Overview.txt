Local Market Place is a free platform that allows local businesses and service providers to easily interact with customers and exchange product and service information. The Web application, developed using HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, ReactJs, and Axios, provides detailed product information and comparisons for stores within a 2-kilometer radius, allowing consumers to make educated shopping decisions. Users may place and fulfill orders, examine extensive service provider information with scheduling choices, receive notifications about new and upcoming shops, and use a product inquiry system for unavailable products. Additionally, the platform provides a quick order and collection service, allowing users to view shop information, make purchases online, and pick them up at certain times without waiting.
